A dedicated Vedic astrologer, grounded in classical method

Nadeem Speaks is a dedicated Vedic Astrologer (Jyotish) with expertise in traditional Vedic astrology, planetary yogas, nakshatras, house analysis and remedial measures. He provides insightful guidance on life's recurring questions — career, marriage, finance, health, education and spiritual growth.

Through personal consultations and educational content, he helps clients understand their birth charts (kundli) and apply practical remedies for positive outcomes. His approach blends classical Vedic principles with real-life application, often incorporating elements of Lal Kitab and palmistry alongside the birth chart.

Philosophy

"Astrology should empower self-awareness, not dependence — I aim to give people practical tools for better decisions, alongside prayer, not instead of it."
